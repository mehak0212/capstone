3D Object Reconstruction using Multiple 2D Images

Why?

There is an increasing need for geometric 3D models in areas such as movies, games, and virtual environments. But the demands are yet to be met, hence we wish to achieve a better understanding of 3D models and how they can be created from 2D images, with less computational cost.

What?

Our purpose is to widen the scope of 3D modeling in the medical world. We will focus on the works which use Deep learning, Computer vision, and Machine learning techniques to estimate the 3D shape of generic objects either from a single or multiple RGB images and create a voxelized 3D model.
